Volatility-Based Stocks

Stocks are categorized based on their volatility. At Invest Wise, we organize stocks into distinct categories based on their volatility characteristics:

High Volatility Stocks: These stocks are known for significant price swings within short periods.

Medium Volatility Stocks: These stocks exhibit moderate price movements compared to highly volatile stocks.

Low Volatility Stocks: Stocks in this category showcase stability with minimal price fluctuations.

Table showing Volatility Index

Volatility Index Description Risk Level Potential Returns
Low Stocks with relatively stable price movement. Low risk Lower returns
Medium Moderate price fluctuations, moderate risk. Moderate risk Moderate returns
High Significant price fluctuations, higher risk. High risk Higher returns
